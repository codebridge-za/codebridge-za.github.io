--- layout: default title: Donate ---

The income from membership fees is enough to cover the basics, but we survive off donations. It can be cash, furniture or time. Here is a current list of some items we're looking for:

Projector

We are currently borrowing a projector for most events, but it would be great to have our own.

Desks and Chairs

We are looking for tressel tables so that we can easily re-arrange the space for large hackathons. We have had a fair number of desks and chairs donated already, but we're always looking for more. Really anything goes - plastic chairs are very useful for overflow seating at events.

Whiteboards

It seems we can never have enough. We ideally want some large wall sized ones, but the smaller ones are useful too.

Your Time

Since we're entirely member-run there are whole lot of random jobs to do around the building (you know - taking out trash, etc.). This website also needs some work (and its on github!)
