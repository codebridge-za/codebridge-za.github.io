The Hacks/Hackers Cape Town re-launch meetup

Hey folks!

We’re getting Hacks/Hackers Cape Town up and running again, and we’re kicking it off with a meeting on Thursday, 19 September at CodeBridge in Claremont.

The first meeting will focus on the future direction of Hacks/Hackers Cape Town. We’ll report on what other Hacks/Hackers chapters are doing, show off the Salary Survey project, see what type of meetings you guys are interested in (talks, hackathons, master classes etc), set a day for regular monthly meetups, explain the setup of Hacks/Hackers internationally and locally, and of course drink some beer and chat to great people.

We’re sorry we’ve been so quiet for so long, but there’s going to be cool, exciting stuff happening in the coming months, and we want you to be part of it.

Date: Thursday 19 September 2013
Time: 6:30pm
Place: CodeBridge, Claremont
Map and directions: http://www.codebridge.co.za/directions.htm
